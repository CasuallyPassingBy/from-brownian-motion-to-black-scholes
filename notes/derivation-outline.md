# Overview of the Derivation
1. Definition of (GBM) geometric Brownian motion.
2. Itô’s formula and SDE for log-price.
3. Construction of a self-financing portfolio.
4. No-arbitrage condition and elimination of stochastic term.
5. Black–Scholes PDE.
6. Change of variables → Heat equation.
7. Fourier transform solution → Closed form.
